For the first time in my life, found myself with nothing planned ahead of me. Hence I'm taking this time to help organize my thoughts.

Technically speaking, this website is supposed to tell people who are interested in finding more about me to, well, find out about me (for whatever reason that I cannot fathom, like you totally could use this time reading my website to go read a [[Favorite Books|book]] instead). Basically tell my story and what not.

After spending an *unreasonably* long time thinking about my story, I've come to the conclusion that I don't really believe in this whole idea. At least, I don't know how to come up with a narrative that can linearly explain my life trajectory. A lot of what I did was purely on a whim, and I didn't do them for the sake of preparing myself for my next role.

I also love [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/), especially being able to connect different notes together. Whenever I am learning something, I always have this urge to fully understand the topic before I can move onto the next one. To me, fully learning something is being able to put this topic in a bigger context, and see how it can relate to stuff I've already learned before. As such, it's kinda obvious why I love using Obsidian.

The purpose of this site is to map out all the different things I've done in life, in a much more non-linear fashion. Some experience will stand out on its own, while others will fit nicely into another. Most of the vault will be notes I've taken in the past, some thoughts I wanted to write down, and maybe even just random drafts I've written. It's all over the place.
