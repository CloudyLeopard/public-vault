**Odds** measure how likely something is to happen compared to it not happening.


$$\text{Odds} = \frac{P(\text{event happens})}{P(\text{event does not happen})} = \frac{p}{1 - p}$$
Where:
- p is the [[Probability (ML)|probability]] of the event (between 0 and 1),
- 1 - p is the probability of the event not happening.
