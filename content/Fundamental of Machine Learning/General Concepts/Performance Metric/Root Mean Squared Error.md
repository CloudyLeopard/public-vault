$$RMSE = \sqrt{\frac{\sum(\hat y - y)^2}{n}}$$
Where
$$\hat y - y = e$$
$\hat y$ = predictions
$y$ = outcome
$e$ = residuals

In other words, RMSE is the standard deviation of the residuals

> [!info] RMSE and [[R-Squared|R^2]] is inversely related

It's also basically the *kind of* the mean of [[Residual Sum of Squares|RSS]], so its more interpretable than RSS
