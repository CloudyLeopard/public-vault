## Concept of Regression

The point of regression is **prediction**

In 1886, Galton realized he could linearly predict the height of children from the height of the parents. He noticed that, using a simple proportion (like Euclid's Rule of 3) led to too extreme predictions (the slope is too steep)

Euclid's Rule of 3 implies a slope of 1. Instead, the actual best-fit line has a slope < 1, showing a **regression to the mean**, and that there is an **optimal slope**.
