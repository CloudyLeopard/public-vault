**Streaming Processor (SP)**: also known as CUDA core
- SPs within [[Streaming Multiprocessor|SM]] share control logic, and instruction cache. That is, SPs are just execute units.

> [!important] Core ≠ [[Core]]
> The core being referenced here is the same as a [[Pipelining|execution unit]]. It is *not* the same as a full fledged [[Core|Core/CPU]]
