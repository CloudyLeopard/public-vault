# Equity

## Analyst Research Report
1. Type company's ticker symbol
2. Press yellow Equity key (on keyboard)
3. type BRC
4. press GO (or Enter) Key

For all research, type RES for Bloomberg Research portal

How to find equity research reports on top 5 firms for some industsry
1. Use CapitalIQ to find firms break down by industry (using **Screening**)
2. Once downloaded, for each firm, use Bloomberg:
	1. DS (Document Search): in "Securities/List", enter the firm ticket. Also, maybe specify the providers i want (good firms)
	2. right click on each document and click "download document"
