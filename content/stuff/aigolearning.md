[AiGoLearning](https://aigolearning.org/) is a nonprofit organization with two main goals:
1. Provide opportunities for children to learn CS and AI at a young age
2. Provide opportunities for HS and College students to teach classes

> *We are students*
> *We are teachers*

![[aigolearning webpage.png]]

Hence, we offer classes for children, and training sessions for aspiring student teachers. We also host annual hackathons, for students to showcase their skills and meet new people.
