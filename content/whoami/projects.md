## VoteEasy

- Created an AI tool to help voters learn more about their elected officials and candidates in election cycles.
- Integrated Google Civic API with FEC data to dynamically map candidates to users based on addresses.
- Fine-tuned language models to predict users’ and candidates’ alignment on political issues, scored on 1-10 scale.
